# Capstone Proposal Template (1 Page)

Keep it concise; bullets are fine. Include a simple diagram.

## Title & One-Liner
- Project name + crisp value statement.

## Problem & Users
- Who benefits? What urgent need?

## Outcomes & KPIs
- Latency, throughput, availability, privacy thresholds (e.g., k ≥ 10, raw TTL ≤ 14d).

## Architecture (Sketch)
- Key services/components; note any alternates.

## Data Model & APIs
- Core schema; 3–5 endpoints (request/response).

## Verification & Trust
- How you promote/demote reports; hoax prevention without censorship.

## Privacy & Ethics (PIA highlights)
- Telemetry decision matrix; minimization; retention; access; disclosure.

## Reliability & Scaling
- Load targets, back-pressure, failure modes and tests.

## Security
- Throttling, IAM boundaries, secrets handling.

## Cost Awareness
- Idle vs surge cost drivers; free-tier strategy.

## Risks & Mitigations
- Top 3 with concrete mitigations.

## Milestones
- Week-by-week goals to demo.
